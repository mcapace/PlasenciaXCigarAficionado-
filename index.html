The Engine: Print Command. Digital Momentum. A Hub That Converts.

We don’t run ads—we stage proof. The back-cover flip becomes the collectible centerpiece; digital surrounds the reader until interest turns into action; the hub ties every chapter together.

The Back-Cover Flip (12 pages)

A branded, clearly labeled mini-issue produced to editorial standards. The flip opens from the back—immediately signaling rarity. Each turn deepens belief: origin, process, signatures, invitation. It looks like it belongs on a coffee table—and that’s the point: **longer life, greater influence, repeated reads.**

The Print System

Spreads and full pages are staged across tentpoles so momentum never drops. The magazine setting confers status; the pacing delivers recall. Buyers don’t just see Plasencia—they carry the story issue to issue.

The Digital System

A single, elegant hub houses every proof point. Native placements and social sequencing direct qualified readers and buyers to it, then retargeting brings them back until they act. **Same story, multiple doors in.**
